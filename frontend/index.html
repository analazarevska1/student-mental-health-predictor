Why this matters

Student burnout is more
common than you think.

Burnout among university students is a growing mental health concern. It builds slowly — through a combination of academic pressure, poor sleep, reduced social connection, and financial worry — often without students noticing until it becomes severe.

MindCheck was built to make self-awareness accessible. It uses a machine learning model trained on real student data to give you an honest, data-driven picture of where you stand — and what you can do about it.
